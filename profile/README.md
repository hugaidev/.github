# HUGAI.DEV

**Building the open standard for AI Competencies.**

Artificial Intelligence is changing how every profession works.

Developers, marketers, recruiters, analysts, designers, lawyers, teachers and healthcare professionals are increasingly collaborating with AI to solve real-world problems.

Yet one fundamental question remains unanswered:

> **How do we objectively measure a person's ability to work effectively with AI?**

HUGAI is an open initiative to define a vendor-neutral standard for measuring AI competencies through observable behaviors and real-world assessments.

Our goal is to build the foundation for a new generation of AI-native education, certification and hiring.

## Our Mission

Create an open standard that enables anyone to:

* Measure AI competencies.
* Design AI assessments.
* Build AI certification paths.
* Compare skills across industries.
* Validate real-world AI capabilities.

## Core Principles

* Human-centered
* Model agnostic
* Tool agnostic
* Domain agnostic
* Evidence based
* Open by default
* Community driven
